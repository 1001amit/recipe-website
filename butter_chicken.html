Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, and all spices except salt.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro.
  7. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
