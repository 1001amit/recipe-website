Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 package pie crusts (2 crusts)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to form a paste.
  3. Add water, granulated sugar, and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  4. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Pour over the sugar and butter mixture.
  5. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter mixture over the crust.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350째F (175째C) and continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
